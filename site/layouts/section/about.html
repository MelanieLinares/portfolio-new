A bit about me

I help brands and agencies ensure accessibility is baked into code and design.

My name is Melanie Linares and I am an accessibility-focused UI/UX developer who has been designing and developing for the web since 2012.

As part of the Level Access delivery team as an Accessibility Consultant I work with cross-functional teams and directly with clients to ensure products are accessible and provide a great user experience, beyond WCAG guidelines.

Previously I was an Inclusion and Diversity Event Lead and Accessibility and Inclusivity Design team member at Insight.
